A life long journey

Ever since I heard the phrase, “ Focus on the journey, not the destination ”, I was immediately sold on the idea. I think that is what attracted me to development. Development is a life long journey evolving with new tools, technologies, better ways to write the language, and so much more. There will always be something new to learn and another way to develop better.

When I'm not coding

I enjoy three pillars that have helped develop me into the character I am now. First is my faith, which has helped me to become a better person and continues to help refine my personal growth. Second is my love of travel. There are so may cultures and destinations to explore. I hope to experience many and maybe all during my lifetime. Lastly is food. I'm a total foodie. Combining my love of food and travel has opened the door to many new and wonderful experiences. I look forward to where this journey called life will lead me.
